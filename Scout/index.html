PRAMUKA: SCOUT OF INDONESIA

Pramuka Activities

In Gerakan Pramuka, we will participate in various fun activities such as Camping (Kemah), Exploration (Jelajah), Marching (Baris berbaris), and Competition (Lomba tingkat). These actvities are fun and targeted to form our own character to become independent and can be very useful as we get many new knowledge. With the 2013 education curriculum in effect, it is compulsory for all Indonesian students to join the scout movement as scouting is officially one of the study units in the curriculum. This contributes to the movement's recognition as the world's largest. It has 21,599,748 members (as of 2011),[2] making it the world's largest Scout association.

Campsite (Kemah)

Campsite (Kemah) is the biggest activity of Scout organizations that is carried out outdoors. Usually the camp is held as an assessment as well as the closing of a series of activities for one year. The activity material in scout camp activities is adjusted to the level of competence that needs to be mastered at a certain level. In general, camping for scouts is useful for practicing independence. By participating in these activities, the character or personality of each member can be seen through each activity and behavior in every situation that is being experienced. As the largest activity of an organization, preparation is needed for its implementation. Preparations for scout camping must also be carried out so that all stages of the activity can run well.

Marching (Baris-berbaris)

In Pramuka, marching is a mandatory material that should be studied. The marching training teaches scouts to live an orderly and orderly life every day. Quoting from the book The Official Scout Guide (2015) by Zuli Agus Firmansyah, what is meant by marching is a manifestation of physical exercise aimed at instilling habits in order to form a certain character. The marching training not only trains the character formation of the scout members, but also trains the physical and forms responsibility. In addition, marching also instills an attitude of discipline and patriotism.

Competition (Lomba tingkat)

Lomba Tingkat, namely a gathering of Scout Raising in the form of a competition in favor of teams or individuals on behalf of the team. Lomba Tingkat is usually abbreviated as LT. The Lomba Tingkat is held in the form of a campsite. The material in the contest is usually grouped into four groups of activities, namely:Religion, Mental, Spiritual and Character Building, which includes; Worship, Spiritual Siraman and ceremonies.Agility, Sport and Patriotism, which includes; sports, obstacle course, pioneering, various sports games gathered into groups and others. Health, Skills, such as; mastery of technical skills (computers, cooking, photography), seminars, sanctity of related processes, scouting, exploration, cipher, semaphore, morse and others. Arts and Culture, such as; cubits of works and crafts, performing arts, bonfires, cultural celebrations, fashion shows and so on.

Exploration (Jelajah)

Exploration/jelajah for pramuka is an interesting, fun and challenging outdoor activity that can develop their love for nature and add insight into the environment and this activity is an educational medium to develop personality and character, for that it is felt that this activity is very important. because with this we can know our nature more closely. In order to improve the quality of Human Resources (HR) it is very important to be followed because with this activity we can make ourselves who love the environment.
